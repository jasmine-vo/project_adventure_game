Print introduction to the game’s story.
Ask the player for their name.
Draw and print map of the rooms.

Start Game:
1. Ask the player what they would like to do or which direction they would like to go.
2. Print the map showing where the user is.
3. Describe the players surroundings.
4. The player enters a command for their next move.
5. Repeat the above steps until the player:
	- Finds their way out, print “YOU WIN!”
	- Runs into a dead end or runs out of energy, print “GAME OVER!”
